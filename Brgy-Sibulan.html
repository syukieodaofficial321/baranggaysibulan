About Our Barangay

Barangay Sibulan Cluster 1 & 2 is dedicated to the development and welfare of its residents. Under the guidance of the Municipality of Sta. Cruz, we strive to maintain peace and order, promote sustainable livelihood, and provide efficient public services to every household.
